How to activate & sign in

Follow these exact steps to activate and sign in safely. If you see unexpected pages or prompts, stop and verify the URL shows activate.uhc.com.

  1. Open a private browser window if you're on a shared device.
  2. Type activate.uhc.com into the address bar (don’t click suspicious links).
  3. Enter the Member ID (from your UHC card), date of birth, and ZIP code.
  4. Create a username and a strong password and click Activate or Continue.
  5. If prompted, complete verification via SMS, email, or security questions.
  6. After activation, sign in at myuhc.com or the UHC app using your new credentials.

Common issue: If the page reloads or shows an error, try clearing cookies or use the "Forgot Username or Password?" flow.

Reset your password

If you can't remember your password, the reset flow usually fixes it quickly without contacting support.

  • Go to activate.uhc.com or myuhc.com.
  • Click Forgot Username or Password?
  • Enter your Member ID or registered email and follow the instructions sent by email or SMS.
  • Choose a new, unique password and save it in a password manager.
  • After resetting, sign in and confirm your contact and recovery options are up to date.

Tip: If the reset message doesn't arrive, check spam/junk folders and any alternate email you might have used.

Security note

Never enter your password on a site you reached from an unexpected email link — always verify the domain is activate.uhc.com.

Recover access

If automated reset doesn't work because your email or phone changed, follow these steps to recover your account:

  1. Go to the official help or "Contact us" link on the activation/sign-in page.
  2. Choose the recovery option and be ready with verification info such as Member ID, recent claim numbers, or billing details.
  3. Support may ask for identity verification — provide only what you're comfortable sharing and only through official channels.
  4. Once verified, update your email and phone in account settings immediately.

If you suspect unauthorized access, change your password immediately and review recent claims and account activity.

Security tips

  • Enable multi-factor authentication if offered.
  • Use a long, unique password for your UHC account — consider a passphrase.
  • Use a reputable password manager to store and autofill credentials securely.
  • Beware of phishing: don’t click links from unknown emails. Type activate.uhc.com manually.
  • Log out from shared devices and remove saved passwords on public computers.

For extra safety, enable alerts for unusual activity if available in account settings.

Troubleshooting & Quick Checklist

Use this checklist to diagnose common problems before contacting support.

  • Is the web address exactly activate.uhc.com?
  • Have you tried a different browser or an incognito/private window?
  • Clear cookies/cache for the site and try signing in again.
  • Try the password reset flow and check all your email accounts for the reset link.
  • If you used employer/sponsor credentials or single-sign-on, try that provider’s sign-in option.
  • Confirm that extensions (adblockers, privacy tools) aren’t blocking necessary scripts.

If none of the above work, gather your Member ID, recent claim or billing details, and the date of last successful activity before contacting UnitedHealthcare support — it speeds verification.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the official activation site?

This is an unofficial guide. The official activation and sign-in pages are activate.uhc.com and myuhc.com.

I forgot my password — what do I do?

Use the "Forgot Username or Password?" link on activate.uhc.com or myuhc.com and follow the instructions sent to your email or phone.

Do I need my Member ID to activate?

Yes — most users need their Member ID, date of birth, and ZIP code to verify their identity during activation.

Can I use the UHC mobile app after activation?

Yes — once your account is activated you can sign in to the UnitedHealthcare mobile app with the same credentials.

Important disclaimer

This is an unofficial help site. For account tasks, always use the official activation page at activate.uhc.com or contact UnitedHealthcare support using the number on your member ID card.
